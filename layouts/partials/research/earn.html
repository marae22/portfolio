WHAT WE GATHERED

Based our synthesis of the data that was provided we came away with a few key insights.

  1. It must be simply and easy to read.
  2. It must be mobile friendly.
  3. It needs to included positive language.

USABILITY TESTING

6 Users | 30 min sessions

During our usability testing we asked our users to take a look at some existing help center pages and give us feedback on what they do and do not like. This gave us a lot of insight to what our users are looking for when using a help center to answer their questions.

After that we had our users test our initial prototype on a mobile device to see what areas we needed to change to make our site the best it could be.
